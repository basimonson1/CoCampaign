# Spare the Dying
*cantrip, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 15 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Choose a creature within range that has 0 [Hit Points](z_Resources/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) and isn't dead. The creature becomes [Stable](z_Resources/CLI/rules/variant-rules/stable-xphb.md).

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The range doubles when you reach levels 5 (30 feet), 11 (60 feet), and 17 (120 feet).

**Classes**: [Cleric](list-spells-classes-cleric); [Cleric](list-spells-classes-cleric); [Druid](list-spells-classes-druid); [Bard (College of Lore)](list-spells-classes-bard-xphb-college-of-lore-xphb)

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 318. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
