# Devil's Sight
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 155*  

**Prerequisite**: 2nd Warlock

You can see normally in [Dim Light](z_Resources/CLI/rules/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) and [Darkness](z_Resources/CLI/rules/variant-rules/darkness-xphb.md)—both magical and nonmagical—within 120 feet of yourself.
