# Attitude
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 361. Available in the Free Rules (2024)* 

A [Monster](z_Resources/CLI/rules/variant-rules/monster-xphb.md) has a starting attitude toward a [Player Character](z_Resources/CLI/rules/variant-rules/player-character-xphb.md): [Friendly [Attitude]](z_Resources/CLI/rules/variant-rules/friendly-attitude-xphb.md), [Hostile [Attitude]](z_Resources/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hostile-attitude-xphb.md), or [Indifferent [Attitude]](z_Resources/CLI/rules/variant-rules/indifferent-attitude-xphb.md).
