# Book
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 25 gp
- **Weight**: 5.0 lbs.

A Book contains fiction or nonfiction. If you consult an accurate nonfiction Book about its topic, you gain a +5 bonus to Intelligence ([Arcana](z_Resources/CLI/rules/skills.md#Arcana), [History](z_Resources/CLI/rules/skills.md#History), [Nature](z_Resources/CLI/rules/skills.md#Nature), or [Religion](z_Resources/CLI/rules/skills.md#Religion)) checks you make about that topic.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 224. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
